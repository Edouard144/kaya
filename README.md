# Kaya

Kaya is an e-commerce platform for hotel and hospitality supplies. Businesses can browse a curated catalog of products — furniture, linens, bathroom fixtures, kitchen equipment, lighting, security systems, and more — and request quotes directly through the site.

## What It Does

- **Product Catalog** — Browse hundreds of hospitality-grade products across 17 categories, from guest room furniture to commercial kitchen equipment.
- **Category Browsing** — Filter by category with instant results. Each category page shows related products with images, descriptions, and pricing.
- **Search** — Case-insensitive product search across the full catalog.
- **Quote Requests** — No checkout cart. Instead, customers submit a quote request with their details. The sales team responds within 48 hours with a tailored offer.
- **Order Tracking** — Registered users can view their submitted quotes and order status in one place.
- **Admin Dashboard** — Admins can add, edit, and delete products, manage categories, upload images, and keep the catalog in sync.
- **Responsive Design** — Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

## Who It's For

Hotels, resorts, serviced apartments, hospitals, and offices looking for a reliable supplier of hospitality-grade furniture, fixtures, and equipment.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React, TanStack Router & Query
- **Backend:** TanStack Start (server functions)
- **Database:** PostgreSQL (Neon) with Drizzle ORM
- **Auth:** JWT-based authentication
- **Deployment:** Vercel
